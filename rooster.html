Rooster Healthcare Solution

Shaping Healthcare's Future

Problem

Rooster Healthcare Solutions identified a
significant challenge for traveling nurses
and doctors. Hospitals often struggle to
find qualified professionals for temporary
roles, while healthcare workers face
difficulties finding reliable job opportunities.
The founder envisioned a platform to bridge
this gap—a website where hospitals could post
job openings and medical professionals
could easily search and apply for those roles.

Solution

The solution was to develop a user-friendly
website that serves as a centralized hub.
Hospitals can post job openings, and
traveling nurses, doctors, and CRNAs
can seamlessly browse and apply
for positions that fit their needs.

My Role

As the designer, I was responsible for
bringing the founder’s vision to life.
I created a modern, intuitive website
design complemented by a cohesive
brand identity, including a logo and style guide.

Scope

This project was completed within a
4-week timeline, covering: Logo design.
Development of a style guide.
End-to-end website design.

Website Designs

My Focus

My primary focus was to design an all-in-one platform that resolves the hiring challenges faced by hospitals and traveling medical professionals. By creating a streamlined and visually appealing interface, the website functions as a comprehensive job hub, simplifying the hiring process and fostering connections between healthcare facilities and qualified professionals.
