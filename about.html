About Me

Hi! I'm a passionate designer with a bachelor's degree in Graphic Design and a minor in Psychology from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My academic choices were driven by a fascination with understanding human behavior, particularly how people interact with technology.

For the past three years, I've been honing my skills at Beeso Studio, working closely with startups and technology companies. As the sole designer, I took on diverse responsibilities, from creating logos and style guides to developing lo-fi and hi-fi designs tailored to client needs. Along the way, I’ve gained invaluable insight into the intricacies of building and scaling businesses.

Outside of work, I stay active by hiking, exercising, and taking long walks in my city's downtown park. I cherish time spent with friends and family and have recently picked up learning Japanese as a new hobby.
