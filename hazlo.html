Hazlo

Hazlo is a digital health application that manages chronic disease
via remote patient monitoring, targeting theLatinX community.

Problem

Hazlo aimed to address the challenges
faced by older individuals in the Latino
community who are struggling with
weight management and diabetes.
The goal was to create both a physical
presence and a digital platform where
users could access personalized schedules
for healthy meals, exercise routines,
motivational content, and health tracking tools.

Solution

To solve this, we developed a web
application tailored to the needs of the target
audience. The platform provides users with
personalized content, including meal plans,
exercise guides, motivational quotes, and
educational articles. Additionally, an admin
portal was built to manage content creation,
user engagement, and partnerships with
health organizations.

My Role

I was responsible for designing the following:
A cohesive logo and style guide to establish
the brand identity. A user-friendly website and
web application for patients.An admin portal for
content creation and management.

Scope

The design process spanned 6-7 months,
covering the project from initial concepts
to final deliverables.

Web App

View on Figma!

Homepage

The homepage is tailored to each user, displaying personalized content such as: Exercise plans. Healthy meal recommendations. Motivational quotes of the day. Articles relevant to their health goals.

Article of the Day

Users receive engaging and educational content, designed to inspire healthier habits and provide actionable health tips.

Admin Portal

The admin portal allows Hazlo to collaborate with health organizations to onboard patients and deliver tailored content.

Questionnaire Creation:

Admins can design and publish questionnaires for patients. Upon registration, users can choose between Spanish or English for their content.

Categories & Interests

Admins can create categories and interests, enabling users to customize their experience by selecting topics relevant to their conditions and preferences.

Headers

Personalizable headers allow admins to organize content into specific sections for patients.

Content Management

Admins can create and attach content to headers, assign titles, and categorize it based on patient conditions and interests.

Mobile Display

View on Figma!

The first screens a patient encounters guide them through the onboarding process. Patients can select their preferred language, log in to the application, and input the invite code provided by their doctor. This ensures a personalized and secure experience tailored to their needs.

Once logged in, patients are directed to their homepage. Here, they can browse and select personalized content, including meal plans, exercise routines, and motivational articles. These features are designed to encourage healthier habits and support their wellness journey.

In this section, patients can view detailed health metrics such as weight, glucose levels, and diagnoses. These metrics are tracked through Wi-Fi-enabled devices that patients use daily, seamlessly syncing with the app. Additionally, patients can upload their insurance cards directly into the platform, eliminating the need for out-of-pocket payments and ensuring streamlined access to healthcare resources.
